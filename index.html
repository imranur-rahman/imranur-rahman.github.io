Hello!

I am a second-year PhD student at NC State University. The main focus of my research interest is Software Supply Chain Security related problems. I am currently working with Dr. Laurie Williams on (a) the exploration of different update metrics across multiple ecosystem, (b) sensitive API usage by different OSS packages (alone and with dependencies) and their potential effect on security advisories or choice of a library from a set of feature equivalent libraries. The whole goal of the second project is to give developers more visibility about what’s going on in their dependencies.
